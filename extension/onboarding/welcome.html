Welcome to EchoKit

RECORD ONCE. MOCK RELIABLY. DEBUG FASTER.
01 / RECORD
Capture real traffic
Open any tab. Click the EchoKit toolbar icon. Press ● REC. Use your app — every fetch and XHR call is captured.
02 / EDIT
Tune your mock
Tap any request. Edit the JSON body, change status, add latency, force errors. Pick a match mode (strict → wildcard → GraphQL).
03 / MOCK
Replay without the backend
Flip the master MOCK switch. Reload the page — your app hits the mocks, not the network. Big amber banner means you never forget.

Keyboard shortcuts (customizable)

Toggle recordingAlt + Shift + R
Toggle MOCK modeAlt + Shift + M
Open popupAlt + Shift + E
Docs & source Privacy
